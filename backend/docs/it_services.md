## WiFi & Network Access

### On-Campus WiFi Networks

| Network | Who It's For | How to Connect |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **WestbrookSecure** | Students, faculty, staff | Use your student email + portal password |
| **WestbrookGuest** | Visitors | Open network; 2-hour sessions, re-authenticate to continue |
| **WestbrookResNet** | Residence hall rooms | Use student credentials; see ResNet setup guide |
| **Eduroam** | Visiting researchers from partner institutions | Use home institution credentials |

### Connecting to WestbrookSecure
1. Select **WestbrookSecure** from WiFi list.
2. Enter your student email (e.g., jsmith@westbrook.edu) as username.
3. Enter your student portal password.
4. Accept the security certificate when prompted.
5. For troubleshooting: help.westbrook.edu/wifi

### VPN
- Required to access certain university systems off-campus (e.g., library databases, HR portal).
- Download Cisco AnyConnect from: it.westbrook.edu/vpn
- Connect to: vpn.westbrook.edu
- Use your student credentials.

## Student Email

- Every student receives a **@westbrook.edu** email address upon enrollment.
- **Format:** firstname.lastname@westbrook.edu (or with number if duplicate: j.smith2@westbrook.edu)
- **Provider:** Microsoft Outlook (via Microsoft 365)
- **Storage:** 50 GB mailbox + 1 TB OneDrive
- **Access:** mail.westbrook.edu or Outlook app (free for students)
- **Setup on mobile:** Configure using your WU email and student portal password.
- **Retention:** Account remains active for 6 months after graduation, then archived.

## Printing

- Print wirelessly from your device using **PaperCut** (install from it.westbrook.edu/print).
- **Free print quota:** 300 black-and-white pages per semester (resets each semester).
- Additional printing: $0.05/page B&W, $0.25/page color; purchased at the library or online.
- Printers available in: Library (LIB 200), ALD 204, SU ground floor.
- **Mobile printing:** Email documents to print@westbrook.edu and release at any campus printer.

## Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

MFA is required for all student accounts to protect against unauthorized access.

1. Download the **Microsoft Authenticator** app on your phone.
2. At first login to the student portal, follow the MFA setup wizard.
3. You will be prompted to verify your identity when logging in from a new device.
4. Backup method: SMS to your registered phone number.
5. Lost access? Contact the Help Desk to reset MFA.
