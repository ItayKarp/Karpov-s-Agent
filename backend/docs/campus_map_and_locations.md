# Campus Map & Locations — Westbrook University

## Academic Buildings

### Alderman Hall (ALD)
- **Location:** North Quad, 100 University Ave
- **Departments:** Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics
- **Notable Rooms:**
  - ALD 101 — Large lecture hall (capacity 250)
  - ALD 204 — CS Teaching Lab (80 workstations)
  - ALD 310 — Math Department main office
- **Hours:** Mon–Fri 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM; Sat 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM

### Brennan Science Center (BSC)
- **Location:** East Campus, 45 Research Drive
- **Departments:** Biology, Chemistry, Physics
- **Notable Rooms:**
  - BSC 100 — Auditorium (capacity 400)
  - BSC 215 – 225 — Chemistry labs
  - BSC 300 — Physics lecture hall
- **Hours:** Mon–Fri 7:00 AM – 11:00 PM; Sat–Sun 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM

### Colton Business School (CBS)
- **Location:** West Campus, 200 Commerce Blvd
- **Departments:** Business, Economics, Finance
- **Notable Rooms:**
  - CBS 102 — Bloomberg Terminal Lab
  - CBS 210 — Case Study Classroom
- **Hours:** Mon–Fri 7:30 AM – 9:00 PM

### Davidson Arts Building (DAB)
- **Location:** South Quad, 30 Arts Circle
- **Departments:** English, History, Philosophy, Fine Arts
- **Notable Rooms:**
  - DAB 105 — Writing Center
  - DAB 200 — Gallery space
- **Hours:** Mon–Fri 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM

## Student Services Buildings

### Student Union (SU)
- **Location:** Central Campus
- **Contains:** Dining hall, bookstore, Career Services, student organization offices, game room, ATM
- **Hours:** Mon–Thu 7:00 AM – 12:00 AM; Fri 7:00 AM – 11:00 PM; Sat–Sun 9:00 AM – 11:00 PM

### Westbrook Library (LIB)
- **Location:** Central Campus, next to Student Union
- See *library_services.md* for full details.

### Wellness & Recreation Center (WC)
- **Location:** South Campus, 5 Wellness Way
- **Contains:** Gym, pool, counseling, health clinic, yoga studio
- **Hours:** Mon–Fri 6:00 AM – 11:00 PM; Sat–Sun 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM

## Campus Shuttle

- **Blue Route:** North Quad → Library → Student Union → South Campus (every 15 min)
- **Red Route:** East Campus → Colton Business School → West Campus (every 20 min)
- **Night Owl:** Central Campus → all dorms (10 PM – 2 AM, every 30 min)
- Schedules: shuttle.westbrook.edu or the WestbrookGo mobile app
