# Clubs & Organizations — Westbrook University

## How to Join a Club

1. Browse the full club directory at orgs.westbrook.edu.
2. Attend an interest meeting (held during the first two weeks of each semester — see schedule below).
3. Fill out the club's membership form (most are online via the club's page).
4. Pay any membership dues if applicable.
5. Show up! Most clubs welcome drop-ins at any meeting.

**Club Fair:** Held the first Friday of each semester, 11 AM – 3 PM in the Student Union Ballroom. All recognized clubs have tables.

---

## Starting a New Club

1. Gather at least **10 interested students**.
2. Identify a **faculty or staff advisor**.
3. Submit a Club Registration Form to the Student Activities Office (SU 220) by Week 3 of the semester.
4. Present to the Student Government Association (SGA) at their next open meeting.
5. Upon SGA approval, the club receives:
   - A university email address
   - Access to room booking
   - Eligibility for SGA funding ($200–$2,000/year based on proposal)
